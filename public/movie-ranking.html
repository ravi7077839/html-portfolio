Money Heist

A Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The series traces two long-prepared heists led by the Professor (Álvaro Morte), one on the Royal Mint of Spain, and one on the Bank of Spain.

The Vampire Diaries

An American supernatural teen drama television series developed by Kevin Williamson and Julie Plec, based on the book series of the same name by L. J. Smith. The series takes place in the fictional town of Mystic Falls, Virginia, a town charged with supernatural history.
